Creative Brief

Client:

Restaurant owner

Restaurant:

Clean Eater’s Plate, a restaurant that serves healthy and organic meals

Project:

Create website for Clean Eater’s Plate

Logo Idea:

someone lifting a weight or a weight + food

Colors/theme:

Greens or oranges (monochromatic colors) with everything simple and well organized

Audience:

People who want a healthy meal or who always eat healthy. People who exercise sometimes or all the time. Could be vegan, vegetarian, or eat meat. People who want to eat well but have the food also taste good. Main focus being: anyone is welcome- don't want a healthy food restaurant that is intimidating.

Competition:

Restaurants in nearby cities, that also serve good and healthy food.

Restaurants in VA:

  1. True Food Kitchen
  2. CHOPT
  3. Tropical Smoothie Cafe
  4. Greens and Grains

Standing out:

Promoting pre-workout meals or post-workout meals, and selling prepped takeout meals.
